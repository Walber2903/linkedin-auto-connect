Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01 Sep 2025

LinkedIn Auto-Connect (Chrome Extension) helps you automate “Connect” clicks on LinkedIn search results pages with conservative limits. This policy explains what data we collect and how it’s used.

Summary

  • We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.
  • All processing happens locally in your browser.
  • We do not use analytics, ads, or any tracking.

Data We Handle

The extension operates only on linkedin.com/search/results* pages and uses a few Chrome Extension APIs:

  • activeTab: runs on the active LinkedIn search tab when you toggle it on.
  • host permissions (https://www.linkedin.com/*): allows the content script to read the page to find “Connect” buttons and click them.
  • scripting: injects the packaged content script on matching pages. No remote code is ever fetched or executed.
  • storage: stores simple local settings (e.g., on/off, debug flag, per-run counters). Not synced, not transmitted.

What We Do Not Do

  • No servers, no telemetry, no third-party SDKs.
  • No collection or transfer of LinkedIn data outside your device.
  • No ads or profiling.

Retention

Only minimal local settings are kept via chrome.storage.local. You can remove them by uninstalling the extension or clearing site data.

User Controls

  • Toggle the extension off in the popup.
  • Disable it on chrome://extensions/ or uninstall it at any time.

Children’s Privacy

This extension is intended for general audiences and does not target children.

Changes
